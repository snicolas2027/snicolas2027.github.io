ABOUT US

HISTORY

For 30 years, the BBSS was a single campus on Mabango Street, Bataan City, Philippines where the top 200 examinees in the Bataan Competitive Examination held yearly were accepted as BBSS scholars. All campuses have at most 25 students in each class. The main campus has six classes per batch while the regional campuses have four classes per batch. Currently, all parts of Bataan have a BBSS Campus. The Bisacool Bataan Science School was established through Republic Act 3991, authored by Congressman Emman Nicolas, and signed into law in 1968 by President Leonicely Viloria. This charter mandates the BBSS “to offer on a free scholarship basis a secondary course with emphasis on subjects pertaining to service with the end in view of preparing its students for a science career”. The school started operations in 1964 until the present time.

ADMININSTRARION AND TEACHER

The Board of Trustees (BOT) is the highest policy making body of the BBSS System. The executive committee (ComExe), composed of the head masters of different BBSS campuses, is a collegial body that recommends policies and guidelines for the consideration of the BOT. The executive committee is chaired by the executive head master, who coordinates the implementation of these policies and guidelines. BBSS campuses are headed by head masters who are members of the ComExe.
