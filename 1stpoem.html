Short Description

My Lover, My Forevermore, a Petrarchan Sonnet, is a poem about lovers who feel like they've spent a lifetime together, making a life together and feeling joyful and vibrant. The poem defines their current mood and lifestyle, as well as what life will be like together in the future. In writing this sonnet, I intended to represent one of the styles and shades of love that is lovely, joyous, and passionate.
